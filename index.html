About Me


I work as a Machine Learning Engineer at Huawei Vancouver Research Center. I earned my Master's degree from Concordia University in Montreal, under the guidance of Dr. Suen.

My research interests and experience encompass a range of topics within machine learning and deep learning, including autonomous driving, model compression, and foundation models. I specialize in model acceleration, utilizing essential techniques such as pruning, quantization, and knowledge distillation. Please refer to my CV for more details.

I am open to new opportunities and collaborations. Feel free to reach out regarding any available positions or projects.
